Our Story

Rooted in the ancient highlands of Ethiopia, Sheba & Co. is the culmination of millennia of culture, hospitality, and craftsmanship. Inspired by the legendary Queen of Sheba, we strive to share a taste of royalty, history, and heart with every cup.

From the first wild coffee cherry to the modern coffee bar, our journey is guided by reverence for origin and pursuit of the extraordinary.

  • • Heritage: Ancient beans, timeless stories
  • • Craft: Every step, hand-finished by artisans
  • • Connection: Every cup brings people together
“In every origin, every story, every ceremony—we are crowned with heritage and fueled by community.”

Sustainability & Impact

Rooted In Respect

We honor the land and the people who give life to every harvest. Our coffee is 100% Ethiopian, sourced from estates that champion biodiversity, fair wages, and generational stewardship.

  • • Regenerative Agriculture: No monocrops, always shade-grown and hand-picked.
  • • Direct Trade: We work directly with farmers for transparency and equity.
  • • Eco-Friendly Processing: Natural sun-drying, minimal water waste.
  • • Women’s Empowerment: Over half our partner farms are woman-led.
  • • Community Reinvestment: Profits help build schools and clean water projects in rural Ethiopia.
“Luxury is nothing without integrity. Sheba & Co. is proud to protect the lands and families that make true coffee possible.”

Explore Our Regions

Sidamo

1,750–2,200m

Known for its vibrant acidity, floral aroma, and delicate lemon notes. Sidamo’s volcanic soil produces coffees with unmatched brightness and body.

Learn More

Yirgacheffe

1,800–2,100m

World-renowned for jasmine aroma, clean citrus notes, and tea-like clarity. The birthplace of “washed” Ethiopian coffee.

Learn More

Guji

1,900–2,300m

Wild, fruity, and cocoa-toned. Guji beans are handpicked by artisan farmers in a lush, untouched region famed for complexity and rich, wine-like flavors.

Learn More

Harrar

1,600–2,000m

The ancient trading route. Harrar’s beans are sun-dried, yielding blueberry, spice, and a full, heavy body—favored by royalty and baristas alike.
